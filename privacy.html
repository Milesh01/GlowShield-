Privacy Policy

How GlowShield collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We believe in complete transparency no legalese.

Last Updated: April 2026
Effective: January 2025
GDPR & CCPA Compliant

Quick Summary: GlowShield does not sell your personal data. We collect minimal information to operate our site. You can request deletion at any time email privacy@glowshield.com.

1. Information We Collect

GlowShield ("we," "our," or "us") collects information you provide directly and information collected automatically when you use glowshield.com (the "Site").

Information You Provide

  • Email address when you subscribe to our newsletter
  • Name and email when you contact us via our contact form
  • Any other information you voluntarily share in messages

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To send our weekly SPF newsletter if you subscribed (unsubscribe anytime)
  • To respond to your questions, comments, or support requests
  • To improve our website content and user experience
  • To analyze site traffic and understand how visitors use our content
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive activity

3. Affiliate Links & Third Parties

GlowShield participates in affiliate programs including Amazon Associates, Sephora, Ulta Beauty, and others. When you click an affiliate link, we may receive a small commission at no extra cost to you.

These retailers have their own privacy policies. When you click through to purchase, their terms apply:

  • Amazon Associates — amazon.com/privacy
  • Sephora Affiliate — sephora.com/privacy-policy
  • Google Analytics — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Email Provider — their respective privacy policies apply

4. Data Retention

We retain your email address for as long as you remain subscribed. Upon unsubscribing, data is removed within 30 days. Contact form submissions are retained for 12 months. Analytics data is retained per Google Analytics default settings (26 months).

5. Cookies

GlowShield uses cookies to improve your browsing experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics traffic patterns (anonymized)
  • Affiliate Cookies: Track referrals to partner retailers for commission

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, though this may affect site functionality.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have these rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent anytime (e.g., unsubscribe)

To exercise any rights, email privacy@glowshield.com. We respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

GlowShield is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, contact us immediately and we will delete it.

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information including HTTPS encryption, limited data access, and regular security reviews. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Newsletter subscribers will be notified of material changes. The "Last Updated" date at the top always reflects the most recent revision. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact Us

GlowShield Privacy Team
Email: privacy@glowshield.com
Response time: 5–7 business days

This Privacy Policy was last reviewed by our team in April 2026. It applies to all visitors of glowshield.com and its sub-pages. By using our site you agree to this policy.
