Why We Exist

Sun protection should be honest, simple,
and work for every skin.

Finding the right sunscreen is genuinely hard especially if you have oily skin, a deeper skin tone, or sensitive skin that reacts to most formulas. Most SPF content online is either outdated, sponsored without disclosure, or written by people who've never actually tested the products.

GlowShield was built to fix that. Every guide, review, and recommendation on this site comes from real testing on real skin across different skin tones, types, climates, and conditions. We're obsessive about ingredient transparency, honest about what doesn't work, and dedicated to making sun protection feel effortless for everyone.

How We Work

Our editorial standards

01
Every product is tested

We never publish a recommendation without at least one team member having used the product on their own skin across multiple days.

02
Affiliate relationships always disclosed

Every page with affiliate links displays a clear notice. We don't hide our commercial relationships in tiny footers.

03
No payment for rankings or coverage

Brands cannot buy their way into our guides. Ranking is determined solely by product performance in our testing.

04
White cast testing is mandatory

Every SPF recommended on GlowShield is tested on at least two different skin tones including deeper tones. White cast performance is always disclosed.

05
Content is regularly updated

Formulas change and new products launch. We review and update our guides at least quarterly to ensure accuracy.
